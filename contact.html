We are a collective of professionals, artists, professors, students, and world travellers who come together to exchange ideas about how to make the world a better place. Our story starts in 2015 when a group of college students won a school competition to launch a non-profit organization.

After living in many different countries through study abroad and service learning experiences, this group of students wanted to share the culture of the countries they visited with people in the United States. They couldn't think of a better way to share the culture of different countries than by helping people volunteer abroad.

As an organization, we believe in the power of volunteerism and support and encourage student learning experiences and overseas volunteering opportunities that strive for the highest standards of child protection and best practice for ethical engagement

If you’re planning a short-term volunteer trip you’re in luck! Our partner organisations overseas have developed some excellent guidance documents and resources to support you in understanding how to avoid any potential harm and instead ensure positive outcomes for vulnerable children, their families and their communities.
